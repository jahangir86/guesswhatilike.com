Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GWIL ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We built GWIL to be a fun, safe game among friends, so we keep data collection to the absolute minimum required to make the game work.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect a username and basic profile details to identify you to your friends.
  • Game Data: We store your Top 3 picks in various categories and the guesses you make to calculate the GWIL Score and enable gameplay mechanics.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous crash logs and basic analytics (like app interaction) to fix bugs and improve the overall user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the game.
  • Calculate GWIL scores between you and the friends you connect with.
  • Fix bugs and improve the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your game picks and guesses are only visible to the specific friends you choose to play with within the app. We only share data if legally required to do so.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for general audiences. We comply with COPPA and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app support page or by reaching out to support@guesswhatilike.com.
